About me

I'm a Computer Science Engineering student at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Coimbatore, pursuing my B.Tech from 2024 to 2028. I build across the stack — from React interfaces down to the microcontrollers and sensors that give a project a physical presence.

My core stack is React, Node.js, and MySQL/MongoDB for the web side, and Java, C, and Embedded C for the hardware side. I've used that combination to ship an e-commerce platform, a stock-trading simulator with real authentication and transaction logic, a Java desktop reservation system, a cooperative multiplayer map-coloring game, and an Arduino-based robot with obstacle avoidance and Bluetooth control.

That same attention to detail carried into research: I'm the first author of a paper on a portable LiDAR-based perception system for four-wheelers in adverse weather, presented at NEleX 2026, an IEEE Madras Section co-sponsored conference hosted by VIT Vellore.

Outside of coding, I've worked with rural communities through Amrita's Live-in-Labs program, and built discipline and teamwork through the National Cadet Corps and National Service Scheme.
